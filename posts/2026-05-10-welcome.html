Welcome

10 May 2026

Welcome to privability.works — a hub for work and discussion on the usability of differential privacy tooling and libraries.

I’m Ravi, a PhD researcher at the University of Bristol. My research evaluates how developers use differential privacy APIs and the tools that support them. I’ve presented at NDSS USEC 2026 and SOUPS, and I have 15+ years experience in cybersecurity.

If you’re interested in differential privacy, privacy engineering, or usable privacy tooling, please join the Discord community: https://discord.gg/bbJQppWjr. Discussions include reproducible research, tooling ideas, library usability, and collaborations.
