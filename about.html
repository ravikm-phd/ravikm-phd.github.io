About

I am a PhD researcher at the University of Bristol focusing on the usability of differential privacy libraries and privacy tooling. I build tools and evaluate how developers and practitioners use differential privacy in practice.

I have presented at venues including NDSS USEC 2026 and SOUPS, and have over 15 years of experience in cybersecurity.

I’m building a community around differential privacy—join the discussion on Discord: https://discord.gg/Y8wq6J8RS.
